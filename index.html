THERE IS POWER IN THE MESSAGE

Hi, you are undeniable

I know it is easy to lose you way. I know life has its challenges. I know it is not always easy to stay strong. However, I do know that every problem has a solution. I do know that there is better days ahead of you.

We made this site because of you. Yes! You! We made this site because you are undeniable! We thought you should know. There is so much talent in the black community. We are artists, politicans, scholars, pastors, athletes, engineers, doctors, lawyers, and business owners. We are mothers, fathers, brothers, and sisters. We are family. We are anything you can think of.

Lord knows the trials that we had had to overcome to get here. That after all the years of being denied. We can now call ourselves leaders. Global leaders. That is an accomplishment. That is undeniable! That is what we want to highlight. The positive things. The real things.

Continue to prosper. You earned it!

Meet Josepha

Trust the process, but do not lose yourself while doing so. Stay focused and keep your eyes on the prize. Always remember where you came from, but never lose sight of where you are going.

-- Josepha Komeka

Meet Ndidi

You live in a world that is against you. From the systems in place, to the people within them. However, we are stronger and bigger than those things, and you have the ability within you to be great and overcome. No matter what anyone says. Keep going. You can do this!

-- Ndidi Chimah

Meet Imani

Do not waste any time. If this year has taught us anything it’s not to take anything for granted. Never think that you’re too young or unqualified to achieve what you want to achieve in life, whether it’s writing a book, applying for a competitive internship/school, or striving for leadership positions within school clubs.

We’re our own biggest enemy when it comes to these things because we constantly tell ourselves that we’re not good enough before even trying, when in reality, the only thing that’s keeping us from achieving that certain thing is ourselves.

-- Imani Mayo

Meet Alex

Meditation is a powerful tool. When you slow down and actively examine your thoughts you can learn a lot. Only thing you need is a calm area to take deep breaths in and out.

-- Alexander Makori

Meet Anthony

The youth are often eager to go out and explore the world; they want to grow up so that they can have access to certain freedoms that adults experience. This is a good trait because it keeps them motivated and optimistic about the future. I also think that the youth population is known for having big, limitless imagination. It enables them to see the world in a different light; it also keeps them from ever getting bored. They look towards the future, create scenarios in their heads, and project their creative ideas onto their futures.

-- Anthony Ibezim

Meet Crystal

My mom recently told me that the key thing people forget is, that we are our own biggest asset. Meaning the most valuable thing we can have in this world, is ourselves. In this day and age it’s easy to get caught up in the materialistic values of the world. It’s easy to want for that new car, that new house, or those new clothes and shoes.How are we going to reach these goals if we aren’t constantly renewing ourselves? If we’re not constantly working on our mindsets and becoming better people, it will reflect in our outside lives.

-- Crystal Ubadiniru

Meet Alexander

Reach out. Sometimes, we must take an introspective look and realize the world doesn’t revolve around us. Don’t take anyone’s presence for granted, as a life can be taken in an instant-as we all have witnessed one way or another this year. Let the outreach be a time to just converse with them, instead of it being a chore. When they-or we-are gone, those seemingly trivial moments are the ones that will be cherished the most down the road. Tomorrow isn’t guaranteed, but the time put into a simple call, or even a text today, is a gift that will last a lifetime.

-- Alexander Bonhomme

Meet Nimah

The endless advice you will receive as you navigate the most confusing, fluid, and uncharted part of your life. Advice will be useful and advice will be irrelevant. People come from a place of love and people come from a place of envy. As you conquer your identity crises and change both your physical and emotional state of being, you will also have to weed through the counsel you will undoubtly receive. Understand that no one has life figured out. Not one person on this earth will ever truly understand their reason for being, until they leave, and at that point we can no longer contact them to ask how it goes.

-- Nimah Nayel
